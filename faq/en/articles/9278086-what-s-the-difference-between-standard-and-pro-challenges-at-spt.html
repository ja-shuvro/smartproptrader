Key Differences Between Standard & Pro Challenges:

  • Profit Target Differences:

    • Whereas our Standard challenges have profit targets of 7% for Phase 1 and 5% for Phase 2, our Pro challenges have profit targets of 8% for Phase 1 and 5% for Phase 2.

  • Drawdown Differences:

    • Whereas our Standard challenges have a daily drawdown of 4% and a maximum drawdown of 8%, our Pro challenges have a daily drawdown of 5% and a maximum drawdown of 12%.

  • Minimum Trading Day Differences:

    • Whereas our Standard challenges have minimum trading days set at 0 days for both Challenge and Funded account types, our Pro challenges have a minimum of 0 trading days for Challenge account types and a minimum of 7 tradings for Funded account types.

Smart Prop Trader believes traders should have a trading journey that is customized to their skill set, which is why we offer traders the optionality of choosing between Standard challenges and Pro challenges. Both challenges offer their unique advantages for traders throughout their trading journey. It's important for traders to choose a challenge that works best with their skill set.

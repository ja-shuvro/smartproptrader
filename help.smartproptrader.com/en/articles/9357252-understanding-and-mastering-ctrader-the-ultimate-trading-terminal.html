cTrader Overview:

cTrader is known as one of the most intuitive and user-friendly trading terminals engineered to deliver an enhanced trading journey complete with customizable parameters that deliver trading advantages to both pros and novice traders. Renowned for its intuitive user interface, cTrader provides a seamless and engaging trading experience for traders of various degrees of skillsets. By choosing cTrader for your trading journey, you unlock numerous advanced parameters, including customizable charting tools for detailed technical analysis, swift order execution, and a versatile interface customizable to individual preferences. Getting Started - Downloading cTrader and Logging In:

Smart Prop Trader affords customers access to cTrader using GooeyTrade.

Once GooeyTrade has been launched, users will need to sign in with their cTrader issued username (email used to purchase your account with Smart Prop Trader) and password. Please note that upon signing up and purchasing your first cTrader account, you will receive an email from Spotware directing you to create a password associated with your email account used at the time of purchasing cTrader. It's important to note that this password will be used to sign into all of your cTrader issued accounts moving forward. When logged into cTrader via GooeyTrade, customers will be able to select from which account they would like to utilize using the upper right-hand section of the GooeyTrade platform as showing in this screenshot:

Please note that Challenge accounts will appear with the word "Demo" listed beside it, whereas Funded accounts will appear with the word "Live" beside it. For users to differentiate between their Phase 1 and Phase 2 account numbers, customers can reference the email that came from Smart Prop Trader upon receiving their Phase 1 or Phase 2 account credentials, entitled "Welcome - Phase # cTrader", or customers can reference their SPT dashboard page located on this page after logging in.
